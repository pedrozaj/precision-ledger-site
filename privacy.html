Privacy Policy

Effective: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how XI LLC ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when operating the Precision Ledger application ("Service"). Precision Ledger is an internal cashflow planning tool; it is not offered as a consumer-facing product.

1. Information we collect

  • Financial account data — via Plaid Inc., when the account owner explicitly connects an institution through Plaid Link. This includes: current balances, available credit, credit limits, transaction history, statement and due dates, account masks (last four digits), account holder name, and institution metadata.
  • Identity & contact — name and email address of the authorized operator using the Service.
  • Operational data — records we manually enter about property taxes, business obligations, invoices, and construction-draw receivables to support planning.

2. How we use information

All data is used exclusively for internal financial planning, reconciliation, and cashflow forecasting for XI LLC and its affiliated entities. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share collected data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. How we share information

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Plaid Inc., strictly as necessary to maintain the account connections that we or an authorized operator have established. Plaid's handling of end-user data is governed by Plaid's End User Privacy Policy.
  • With Google LLC, for authentication (Google Workspace SSO) and for Gmail/Calendar integration limited to accounts owned by XI LLC.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of XI LLC and others.

4. Data storage and security

  • All data is stored on systems controlled by XI LLC and restricted to authorized personnel via multi-factor authentication and role-based access.
  • Access tokens issued by Plaid are stored in restricted local configuration files and are not committed to source control or shared.
  • Data in transit is protected using TLS 1.2+.
  • We review access to the Service on a periodic basis and revoke credentials upon departure or role change.

5. Data retention and deletion

Financial data is retained only for as long as needed to support ongoing planning and reconciliation. You may request deletion of any data we hold by emailing jpedroza@thex1.com. Upon request, we will delete the relevant records and revoke any associated Plaid access tokens within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law (for example, tax or audit obligations).

You can also revoke a Plaid connection at any time from the Precision Ledger dashboard or directly from Plaid's my.plaid.com portal. Revocation prevents any further data retrieval.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the California Consumer Privacy Act), you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information; to request a copy of the information we hold about you; and to opt out of any sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information). To exercise these rights, email jpedroza@thex1.com.

7. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated directly to connected operators. The "Effective" date at the top of this policy reflects the most recent revision.
