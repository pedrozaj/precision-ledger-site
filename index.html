The single source of truth for money owed, money coming in, and every date in between.

Precision Ledger is an internal cashflow command center used by XI LLC to consolidate personal and business financial obligations — credit-card balances, business loans, property taxes, open invoices, rental income, and construction-draw reimbursements — onto one date-driven timeline.

Money owed

Credit cards (personal and business), business loans, property taxes, franchise taxes, and state annual filings — each with its own due date, statement cycle, and minimum payment. Aggregated for quick triage.

Money coming in

Open invoices from QuickBooks, rental income, client AR, and bank draws on active construction projects — projected against the next 90 days so you know which weeks are tight and which are flush.

Date-driven timeline

Every obligation and every receivable on one calendar. No more missed filings, no more surprise autopay charges, no more forgotten construction draw submissions.

How data is sourced

Precision Ledger connects to financial institutions via Plaid. With your explicit consent at the point of connection, we retrieve current balances, credit limits, statement and due dates, and recent transactions. The data is used strictly for internal planning and reporting — it is not shared, sold, or resold. You can disconnect any institution at any time from the Precision Ledger dashboard, which revokes the access token.

For full details on data collection, storage, retention, and deletion, see the Privacy Policy.
